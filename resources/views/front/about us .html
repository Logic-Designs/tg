TG Developments is an esteemed Egyptian joint stock company with over 20 years of extensive experience in the field of real estate development and investment owned by Mr. Ashraf Tahoon.

TG Developments is currently investing about 8 Billion EGP in various locations in Egypt.

With a primary focus on delivering high-quality developments, By offering diverse real estate options, TG Developments aims to provide individuals and businesses with exceptional spaces that meet their specific requirements.

The company's extensive experience and focus on quality and innovation, position it as a leading player in the development and investment sector.
